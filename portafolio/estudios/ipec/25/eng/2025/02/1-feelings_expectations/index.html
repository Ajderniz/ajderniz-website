My feelings and expectations about this course

2025-02-11 by Ajderniz, tagged as cotidiano

1. Feelings

I was pleasantly surprised when I learned that this was not going to be another endless cycle of learning the VERBO TU 'BI. As I understood it, we are rather to be taught several aspects relating to the IT context, under different topics, through an immersive/input language learning method. We have content, organization, and a method!

The teach' does this thing where he uses his body language to convey the messages says in English, thus helping everybody understand, through context and demonstration, the general--and not so general--idea.

He did this during his first assignment. First, he wrote on the whiteboard the instruction for what to do; then, he went on to say out lout each and every instruction, whilst making sure he was understood by every class student.

2. Expectations

What do I expect for this course? First and foremost, I hope to learn information that I haven't learned; otherwise, it would be lost time, and lost effort.

As to what I personally expect for myself, I expect to be pushed into finally gittin' gud at networking and so on, always ensuring that I am the one who controls my own stuff.

By the way, I don't expect to be this verbose about every single entry I do. I'm just a bit excited today, that's all. My usual output is very minimal if I don't feel like it's worth the effort to write about something.
